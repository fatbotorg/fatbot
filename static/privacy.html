Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Your privacy is important. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it to help you stay active.

1. Information We Collect

To operate the bot effectively, we collect the following limited information:

  • Telegram User Info: ID, Username, and Display Name (to identify you in the group).
  • Workout Media: Photos and videos you upload to the bot for verification.
  • Integration Data: If you connect Strava or Whoop, we receive workout summaries (duration, calories, type).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To track your compliance with the 5-day workout rule.
  • To generate weekly group reports and leaderboards.
  • To verify workout authenticity using OpenAI (for image analysis).
  • To send you reminders before deadlines.

3. Data Sharing

We only share data in two specific ways:

  • OpenAI: Images are sent to OpenAI for automated analysis to confirm they are valid workouts.
  • Group Transparency: Your workout stats and leaderboard position are visible to other members of your Telegram group.

4. Your Rights

You can stop using the bot at any time by leaving the Telegram group. If you wish for your historical data to be deleted, please contact the bot administrator directly.
